Vocabulary
- on Hit
  landing any attack on a target
- on Crit
  successfully performing a critical strike animation on stance-broken, guard-broken, parried or back-stabbed enemies
- on Two-Handed Hit
  landing two-handed non-paired weapon Attacks
- on Final Hit
  _landing attack(s) at the end of a light attack chain on a target
- on Charged Attack Hit
  _landing a charged strong attack on any target
- on Rapid Attacks
  landing several Attacks in a specific time frame enables "rapid attack" status for 2 seconds
- while Sprinting
  includes the sprint performed when holding the evade key
- while Decaying
  while suffering from poison or scarlet rot
- when exposed to Decay
  while suffering from poison or scarlet rot, or when poison or scarlet rot is inflicted close by
- Swordart
- Character Skill
  includes Spring-loaded Whirlblade's projectile
- Character Ability
  Nightfarer's Activated Ability and Ultimate Art. Includes Revenant's Relic Ghostflame Explosion
- Mana Ability
  Sword Arts, Sorceries & Incantations
- Sword Arts
  Weapon Skills/Ashes of War/Sword Arts
